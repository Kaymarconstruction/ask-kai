Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Ask Kai collects, uses, and stores user data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address for login and account access
  • Prompt usage to track plan limits
  • Optional supplier contact details (Settings)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize your experience with Ask Kai
  • To maintain secure user sessions
  • To contact users if required for support or account updates

3. Data Retention

User data is stored securely via Supabase. You may request account deletion by emailing support@kaymarconstruction.com.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Stripe for payments and OpenAI for generating responses. These platforms have their own privacy policies.

5. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact support@kaymarconstruction.com.
